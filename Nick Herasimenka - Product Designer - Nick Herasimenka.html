I’m a San Francisco based designer from Belarus. I designed digital products for Apple, Mastercard, Famous, Google, Adidas, Edmunds & more. I create experiences across all phases of product design from ideation to high-fidelity UI and interaction. I always strive for a simple aesthetic solution, usability, and beauty. JINS

Founded in Japan in 2001, JINS is a leader in crafting stylish, high-quality and innovative eyewear at affordable prices. JINS operates more than 340 retail stores in Japan and China and opened its first U.S. store in San Francisco in April 2015.

Target

Target is a fast turnaround freelance project I did back in 2015. The activity tracking application for ios and WatchOS. The app has pretty simple functionality, it runs in the background all the time and keeps track of your steps you & burning calories.

JOIN

JOIN is the first telecommunication operator offering integrated IT solutions and is the first offering a European tariff equal to a national communication. The company offers free outgoing and incoming calls, texting and the internet.

Edmunds

Edmunds.com is an American online resource for automotive information. In addition, the company builds inexpensive applications for small and large dealerships across the country.

Coachfrog

Coachfrog.ch simplifies access to qualified therapists for people with health concerns. The website provides an online marketplace for both professionals and patients. It gives personal market space for the first and universal search and booking tool for the second.
